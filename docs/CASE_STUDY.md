# Case Study: How Agentic Nexus Helps Teams

## Scenario
A small consulting company handles requests across finance, legal, technology, and media. Before Agentic Nexus, they switched tools and experts for each request, which caused delays and inconsistent recommendations.

## Challenges

- Fragmented workflows across multiple domains
- Slow response times for first drafts
- No unified conversation memory for authenticated users
- High overhead in handoffs between team members

## Solution with Agentic Nexus

Agentic Nexus provides six specialist AI agents in one interface:

1. Economics
2. Finance
3. Technology
4. Medical
5. Law
6. Media

With Supabase authentication and persistence, signed-in users get cloud-stored conversation history. This means teams can return to prior discussions and continue from context.

## Workflow Improvement

- User signs in with magic link
- User asks a domain question
- Agent auto-routes or uses selected specialist
- Response is generated and persisted
- Team reopens app later and continues the same thread

## Business Impact

- Faster first-response drafts
- Better continuity across sessions
- Less context loss in expert handoffs
- Lower operational friction for multi-domain support

## Why it matters

Agentic Nexus shifts teams from disconnected, one-off prompts to a persistent, role-based intelligence workspace.
